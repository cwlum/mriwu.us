Privacy Policy

How your data is handled.

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

Welcome to mriwu.us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information You Provide Voluntarily: When you use our contact form, we collect the information you provide, such as your name and email address, to respond to your inquiries. For administrative purposes, we also collect login credentials from authorized users for site management.
  • Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our website, we may use session cookies to maintain user sessions for authenticated administrators. We do not collect personal data such as IP addresses or browsing history from general visitors for tracking purposes.
How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and messages submitted through the contact form.
  • To operate, maintain, and manage our website, including authenticating administrators.
  • To improve the user experience and content of our website.
Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your information is processed internally by our server for the purposes described above. We may disclose your information only if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. Administrative passwords are encrypted, and we follow industry best practices. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Cookies

This website uses cookies for essential functions. Specifically, we use session cookies to manage administrator logins. These cookies are necessary for the security and functionality of our site's administrative areas. We may also use third-party services like Google Fonts, which may place their own cookies. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent, but some portions of our site may not function properly as a result.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us (e.g., social media links, Google Fonts). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact form on our website.
